1. Introduction

Welcome to Social Media Dashboard ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Important Notice

This privacy policy applies to the Social Media Dashboard application and describes how we handle data from Facebook and Instagram when you use our service.

By using our application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Facebook Data

When you connect your Facebook account, we may collect:

  • Basic Profile Information: Your name, email address, and profile picture
  • Page Data: Information about Facebook pages you manage, including follower counts and engagement metrics
  • Public Profile Data: Publicly available information from your Facebook profile

2.2 Instagram Data

When you connect your Instagram account, we may collect:

  • Basic Profile Information: Username, account type, and profile information
  • Follower Metrics: Number of followers and following
  • Media Information: Count and basic metadata of your posts

2.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our application:

  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • Usage patterns and interaction data
  • Log data (IP address, access times, pages viewed)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

Analytics & Display
  • Display your social media follower counts
  • Show engagement metrics and growth statistics
  • Provide dashboard functionality
Service Operation
  • Maintain and improve our application
  • Provide customer support
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
Data Minimization Principle

We only collect and process the minimum amount of data necessary to provide our service. We do not use your data for advertising purposes or sell it to third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Share Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your social media data remains private and is only used to provide the dashboard functionality you requested.

4.2 Limited Exceptions

We may disclose your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or court order
  • Safety: To protect the rights, property, or safety of our users
  • Business Transfer: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale

4.3 Service Providers

We may employ third-party companies to facilitate our service. These third parties have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for other purposes.

5. Data Security

Encryption

All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols.

Secure Storage

Data is stored on secure servers with restricted access.

Access Control

Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms.

Security Limitation

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

6.2 Account Disconnection

You can disconnect your Facebook and Instagram accounts at any time:

  • Use the logout function in our application
  • Revoke permissions in your Facebook settings
  • Contact us to permanently delete your data

6.3 Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our service:

  • Active Use: Data retained while you use our service
  • After Disconnection: Data deleted within 30 days
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law
