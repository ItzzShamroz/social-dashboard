How to delete your data from Social Media Dashboard

Complete guide to removing your information from our service

Overview

We respect your right to control your personal data. This page provides clear instructions on how to delete your data from our Social Media Dashboard application.

Your Data Rights

Under various privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), you have the right to request deletion of your personal data. We provide multiple ways to exercise this right.

Step-by-Step Deletion Process

1
Disconnect from the App

First, disconnect your Facebook and Instagram accounts from our application:

  • Open the Social Media Dashboard
  • Click your profile picture in the top-right corner
  • Select "Logout" from the dropdown menu
  • This will immediately disconnect your accounts
Note: Logging out stops all data collection but doesn't delete existing data.
2
Revoke Facebook Permissions

Remove our app's access from your Facebook settings:

  • Go to Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  • Find "Social Media Dashboard" in your app list
  • Click "Remove" to revoke all permissions
  • Confirm the removal when prompted
Important: This prevents future access but doesn't delete stored data.
3
Request Complete Data Deletion

To permanently delete all your data from our systems:

Email Request

Send to: privacy@socialmedia-dashboard.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Include:

  • Your name and email address used with our service
  • Facebook/Instagram usernames (if comfortable sharing)
  • Request for complete data deletion
  • Any specific data concerns
4
Confirmation and Timeline

After submitting your request:

  • Acknowledgment: We'll confirm receipt within 24 hours
  • Verification: We may ask for identity verification
  • Processing: Deletion completed within 30 days
  • Confirmation: You'll receive final confirmation email
What Gets Deleted
  • Profile information
  • Social media metrics
  • Usage logs
  • Account preferences
What May Remain
  • Legal compliance records
  • Security logs (anonymized)
  • Aggregated analytics
  • Support communications

Important Information

Before You Delete

Please consider the following before requesting data deletion:

  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • You'll lose access to all historical data and analytics
  • Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes
  • You can always reconnect later, but historical data won't be recovered
